# Monster Ranch Trail Manual

Genre: weekly creature ranch sim.

## Overview

Monster Ranch Trail is about building a small ranch, caring for creatures, training carefully, exploring zones, upgrading facilities, and entering tournaments without burning out the herd.

## Goal Of A Run

Raise the ranch tier and league progress while keeping creatures fed, healthy, bonded, and rested. A good ranch grows slowly; an impatient ranch creates fatigue and injuries.

## Screen Guide

### RANCH

RANCH is the weekly planning board. Check weather, money, feed, meds, reputation, bond, fatigue, tier, and the next pressure point.

### CREATURE

CREATURE focuses on species and condition. Spriglets, cinderpups, pebblors, mirelings, fusefoxes, quilloxes, snowcalves, moonmoths, and chrome pups all represent ranch collection and care.

### TRAIN

TRAIN improves power, grit, speed, focus, and bond. Training advances progress but raises fatigue.

### EXPED

EXPED sends the ranch into pine, warm, mire, glass, fuse, snow, moon, and chrome zones. Expeditions produce discoveries and risk.

### BUILD

BUILD upgrades stable, clinic, yard, garden, workshop, nursery, lookout, vane, and pen facilities.

### TOURNEY

TOURNEY enters novice, harvest, storm, rival, frontier, and champion cups.

### EVENT

EVENT resolves creature refusals, storms, rival moments, cures, injuries, bonds, eggs, and filter trouble.

## Core Loop

1. Check RANCH each week.
2. Care for creatures and reduce fatigue.
3. Train when the herd can handle it.
4. Explore for discoveries.
5. Build facilities before harder tournaments.
6. Enter cups when stats and fatigue are ready.

## Risk, Progress, And Endings

Fatigue is the central danger. Bond, meds, and facilities protect the herd. Progress and reputation unlock stronger outcomes such as league winner, cozy ranch, variant archive, rival friend, research star, or empty barn.

## Tips For First Run

- Rest after hard training.
- Keep feed stocked.
- Upgrade clinic or stable early.
- Do not enter every tournament as soon as it appears.
- Bond helps smooth bad events.

## Offline Fictional Scope

All creature care, training, ranching, expeditions, and tournaments are fictional offline game content. This manual is not real animal-care guidance.
